Why We Built ListValet

Selling platforms have spent billions of dollars to make selling easier. Yet, there still remains a disconnect between people with stuff and the selling platforms. We call this disconnect: the gap. Evidence of this gap can be seen in our garages, basements, closets, and storage units. In fact, an average home in the U.S. has $3,100 of unused items. That’s $391 billion dollars’ worth of items collecting dust, depreciating in value, and turning into waste.

This gap within the resale market is caused by a person’s reluctance to sell an item for one reason, or possibly many. Here are a few:

  1. Too busy
  2. Value of time > value of item
  3. Laziness
  4. Scams & Fraud
  5. Endless calls, texts, messages & spam.
  6. Meetups with strangers
  7. Not proficient at selling
So how do you cure one’s laziness? Or make up for a person’s lack of time? Or prevent scams or potentially dangerous meetups with strangers? It’s simple – hand over the task of selling to someone else. And that’s ListValet!

ListValet is a platform that bridges the gap by connecting people to local sellers – also known as Lystants (listing assistants). Lystants make selling easy, hassle-free, and safe. Their service includes: picking up items, posting them across several platforms, finding buyers, and selling the items.

The best part about ListValet? Everybody wins.

People Win – More time to spend with friends, family, and hobbies. Recuperate investments from unused items. Less clutter and cleaner homes. Safety and personal information uncompromised.

Sellers Win – Students, stay-at-home parents, and others can earn supplemental income. Satisfaction from providing a valuable service. Meet and communicate with new people.

Selling Platforms Win – Better sellers using the platforms. Higher quality listings. More items posted onto marketplaces. Better overall experience for buyers.

(BOLD)Buyers Win(BOLD) – More items to purchase. Better experiences dealing with sellers from Lystant.

Our vision is comprised of four goals:

(1) Make selling effortless,

(2) Promote the recycle of goods through resale,

(3) Improve the overall quality of all selling platforms,

(4) Create supplemental income for hundreds of thousands of people.

Our vision of making a lasting impact on the world leaves us motivated and energized. Visit us at ListValet to learn more.

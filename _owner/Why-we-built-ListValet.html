Why We Built ListValet

Selling platforms have spent billions of dollars to make selling easier yet there still remains a disconnect between people with items and the selling platforms. We call this disconnect the gap and evidence of this gap can be seen in our garages, basements, closets, bedrooms, offices, spare rooms, and storage units. There are currently 128.58 million households in the United States and each household has an average of 50 unused items valuing $3,100. That’s 6.42 million items valued at $398 billion just sitting around in the average home taking up space, collecting dust, depreciating in value, and turning into waste.

This gap within the resale market is caused by a person’s reluctance to sell an item for one reason or another. Here are a few examples:

  1. They're too busy and don't have the time to move these unused items
  2. Their time is more important to them than selling the item
  3. Their laziness and procrastination takes over
  4. The fear of scams & fraud keeps them from trying
  5. Dealing with endless calls, texts, messages, & spam
  6. Don't have the desire to meet with strangers
  7. Not proficient enough at selling

So how do you cure one’s laziness? Or make up for a person’s lack of time? Or prevent scams or potentially dangerous meetups with strangers? It’s simple – hand over the task of selling to someone else. And that’s what ListValet is all about!

ListValet is a platform that bridges the gap by connecting people to local sellers who make selling easy, hassle-free, and safe. It will provide the means for you to connect with an eager and experienced seller who will take care of all those irritating problems that comes with selling an item. They'll advertise it on multiple platforms, they'll deal with potential buyers, they'll handle the time it takes to sell the item and all you have to do as the owner is settle on an agreement with the seller while they take care of all the inbetween. Simple as that!

The best part about ListValet? Everybody wins!

People win – By selling your unwanted and unused items through ListValet you won't have to deal with the hassle and headache of selling the items yourself. This will give you more free time to spend with family or friends or on hobbies and passions. It will declutter your home and you'll feel refreshed and more eager to tackle those projects you now have the time for. And your mind will be at ease because you and your personal information will be safe and uncompromised.

Buyers win – More people using ListValet to sell their unused and unwanted items means there will be an influx of items in multiple marketplaces available for anyone that is interested in buying. More items means more buyers and sellers which means more options to choose from and which will provide something to suit everyone's needs.

Sellers win – This service is available to anyone no matter your walk of life. Students, stay-at-home parents, full-time workers, anyone interested in a supplemental income and an eagerness to meet new people and develop a valuable set of skills can enjoy selling through ListValet.

Selling platforms win - Having more people on ListValet means there will be more users of selling platforms as a whole as sellers will be looking for any potential ways to sell the goods entrusted to them. More people using ListValet means more people using Facebook Marketplace, Amazon Marketplace, Craigslist, and more.

ListValet's vision is a desire and eagerness to improve everyone's lives and we hope to achieve that through these four, simple goals:

  1. Promote the recycling of goods through resale
  2. Make selling as simple and as effortless as possible and remove the stress involved
  3. Create supplemental income and an enjoyable way to develop new skills for hundreds of thousands of people
  4. Improve the overall quality of all selling platforms from ListValet to Facebook Marketplace

By reaching these goals and attaining its vision, ListValet is helping impact the world by providing people with the means to make additional income, clear space out of their homes and remove the stress of selling, and provide them with a feeling of happiness and satisfaction.
